What is Katoto Project?

Katoto Project, a non-governmental organization (NGO) was founded on June 26, 2020, and later recognized by the Municipal Council of Paete on March 2, 2022, through Resolution Blg.20.t.2021, Accreditation No. 2021-049. We are also acknowledged by the Youth Development Affairs Office of Laguna as a youth organization of the Provincial Government. A year after our establishment, we were honored as one of the "Sampung Sibol ng Laguna 2021 and 2022" in the Gawad Kampeon ng Kabataan ng Laguna, a program by the Provincial Government, YDA, and Gawad Laguna Inc. Additionally, the Good Gov PH bestowed upon the Katoto Project the Platinum Award for the Seal of Good Youth Governance, the highest recognition given to organizations exemplifying excellence and cleanliness in governance. As our journey continues, we have achieved further recognition, such as the Outstanding Volunteer of CALABARZON AND PHILIPPINES (National Awardee) from NEDA and PNVSCA, Most Outstanding Youth Social Enterprise in the 2022 Villar SIPAG Awards: Youth Poverty Reduction Challenge, and 3rd Place in the Emerging Tech4Ed Centers of CALABARZON, DICT D4.
